🚨 Incident #104 — Missing Patient Invoices

Incident Alert

53 patient visits completed but no invoices generated.

Step 1 — Detect missing invoices

SELECT patient_id, visit_date
FROM visits
WHERE invoice_id IS NULL;

Finding: 53 visits without invoice detected.

Step 2 — Check billing jobs

SELECT *
FROM billing_jobs
WHERE status = 'FAILED';

Finding: Billing job failed after system update.

Root Cause

Timezone mismatch caused billing job to skip records.

Resolution

  • Fixed timezone parameter
  • Reprocessed billing job
  • Invoices generated successfully
