Incident #104 — Missing Patient Invoices

HIGH SEVERITY HEALTHCARE ERP

Context

Hospital reported missing invoices after completed patient visits.

Impact

Revenue inconsistency detected in billing module.

Investigation

Step 1 — Missing invoices detection
SELECT patient_id, visit_date FROM visits WHERE invoice_id IS NULL;

53 records found

Step 2 — Billing system check
SELECT job_id, status FROM billing_jobs WHERE status = 'FAILED';

Batch processing failure detected

Root Cause

Timezone mismatch in batch processing system.
